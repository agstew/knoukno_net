About Kno U Kno

Know you know — and build from there.

Starting a business has never been more possible — or more confusing. There are powerful tools for payments, storefronts, accounting, marketing, AI, legal docs, and more. But tools aren’t a path, and the internet’s “advice firehose” can make your first step feel riskier, not safer. Kno U Kno exists to close the gap between what you already know and what you need to do next. We translate the messy beginning into a practical sequence of actions that fit your goals, your location, and your time.

The name means exactly what it says: you know more than you think. You know a skill, a community, a problem worth solving, or a way of showing up that people remember. We start with that center of gravity and build a business around it — one that is small, real, and revenue-seeking from day one. Instead of a 60-page plan that gathers dust, you’ll leave each week with two or three moves that advance the only metrics that matter: conversations, commitments, and cash.

How it works

When you join, we ask a few questions about your idea, your location, and how much time you can invest. The platform creates a personalized start plan with milestones and due dates. Each milestone is a compact playbook: what to do, why it matters, the template to use, and how to know you’re done. If your state requires registration or a permit, we surface the exact steps and link directly to the official forms. If your industry has customary pricing or compliance norms, we explain those in plain language and show examples.

You’ll run short customer discovery sprints — five conversations, not fifty — with scripts that make it easy to listen, not pitch. You’ll assemble a simple offer page that states your promise, your price, and how to buy. You’ll learn the finance basics that actually change outcomes: separating accounts, tracking gross margin, and making a weekly cash plan. When you’re ready, we outline your first channels — usually the ones nearest to you: referrals, partnerships, local listings, or a single social platform that matches your audience.

What you get

  • Personalized Start Plan — milestones that fit your state/industry and time budget.
  • Formations & Compliance — plain-English guides with links to official pages.
  • Templates — outreach scripts, offer pages, invoices, and pricing calculators.
  • Weekly Momentum — reminders and check-ins that keep progress visible and doable.
  • Community & Coaching — feedback from founders a few steps ahead.

Hard truths we embrace

It is harder to start now because customers have endless options and attention is scarce. That’s why we push for small, fast tests and clear promises. It’s harder because legal and financial decisions come earlier than you expect; we meet that head-on with guidance and defaults that are sensible, not scary. It’s harder because energy fluctuates; we design for consistency over intensity, so a busy week doesn’t erase your momentum.

Our promise

We can’t guarantee outcomes, but we guarantee clarity. Every step you take with Kno U Kno will be specific, sized to your week, and connected to revenue. You’ll always know what to do, how to do it, and how to tell if it worked. When you hit friction, you won’t be alone — you’ll have a map, tools, and people who want you to win. The result isn’t just a business that can earn; it’s confidence you can carry into every next step.

When you’re ready to begin, return to the Landing and tap “More …” — or simply create your account. You already know enough to start. We’ll help you prove it.
