Privacy Policy Privacy‑first

Last updated: 2025-10-26

Who we are

Kno U Kno helps you go from idea to business with guided questions, grading, and progress tracking. We design the product to collect the least data needed to operate.

Scope

This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to our website, app, and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

What we collect

  • Strictly necessary data — session state, security, and consent storage (cookies/local storage). These are required for the Service to function.
  • Account data — name, email, login info you provide.
  • Content you create — your answers, ratings, averages, and exported results.
  • Payments — processed by our payment provider (e.g., Stripe). We receive payment status and identifiers; your full card details are never stored on our servers.
  • Optional analytics/marketing — only if you opt‑in via our cookie preferences.

Lawful bases (GDPR)

  • Contract — to create your account, deliver Starter/Member features, and support you.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the Service secure and prevent abuse.
  • Consent — for analytics and marketing cookies/processing (not enabled by default).
  • Legal obligation — to comply with tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements.

How we use data

  • Operate, secure, and improve the Service
  • Provide paid features and receipts
  • Respond to support requests
  • With consent, understand usage (analytics) and offer relevant updates (marketing)

Cookies and local storage

Strictly necessary — login/session, security, consent storage (always on)
Analytics — usage metrics (only if you accept)
Marketing — campaign attribution and personalization (only if you accept)

You can manage your choices anytime: Cookie preferences. Clearing browser data will reset your consent.

Your rights

GDPR (EEA/UK) users may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to processing, and data portability. US (CPRA/CPA/CTDPA/etc.) users may have the right to know, delete, correct, and opt‑out of sale/share of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise rights, contact us at privacy@knoukno.example. We may verify your request.

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, then delete or anonymize it. You can request deletion of your account data at any time.

Security

We use industry‑standard security measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our defenses.

International transfers

When data is transferred internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

Processors

We use trusted processors to provide parts of the Service. Key processors may include:

  • Stripe — payments
  • Hosting — cloud infrastructure and storage
  • Email — transactional email delivery

We will update this list if material changes occur.

Children’s privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the Service or laws. We will post the updated date at the top of this page.
